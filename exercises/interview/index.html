An Interview with Erin Hayes

Zoe What is your favorite nickname that you’ve been given?
Erin I have a complicated relationship with nicknames, actually. The only one that has stuck is Hayes, my last name, but even then it hasn’t really felt like MY nickname. My favorite though would probably be Hazelnut, but it is longer than my actual name, so I’m not sure it really counts haha
Zoe
What's the best piece of advice you've ever been given?
Erin
Think about your knowledge like a bubble. Increase the surface area of that bubble by exploring in one direction until you get bored and want to go somewhere else. Then follow your interests to another corner and expand your knowledge there for a while. Continue this process your whole life, always expanding the surface area of your bubble. Never settle into the comfort of doing what you already know.
